### Examination committee.

Examination Committee is a department comprised of two members appointed by institute’s administration. The committee examines and supervises the question papers of both midterm and annual examinations. It takes the responsibility of supervising the examination, closely guards the question papers. And it checks closely the markings of exam papers. Except for the title of Khenpo, the committee supervises in awarding certificates of Madyama, Shastra and Acharya degrees and looks after all examination related works.
