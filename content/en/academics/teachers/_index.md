All of the teachers who are teaching at this Monastic college are immediate or subsequent disciples of late Khenchen Kunga Wangchuck. They all have finished studying the 'Five great subjects' of the Sutric Buddhism, and have also been initiated with the ripening empowerments of the four classes of Tantra and endowed with liberating instructions. All of them perfectly observe the commitments and their vows.

The teachers are selected by Kyabje Khyentse Rinpoche, Khen Rinpoche and the Shedra's administration, examining each candidate's academic knowledge, personal character, and outlook. A selected student is first given a post of review teacher (skyor dpon), then promoted to full teacher, and eventually made Khenpo. The teachers are very experienced in teaching; many have attended modern teacher-training workshops under Ms Das and her colleagues, organised by the Khyentse Foundation.
