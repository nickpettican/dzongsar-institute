Following the vision and guidance of His Eminence Khyentse Rinpoche, Dzongsar Khyentse Chökyi Lodrö Institute regularly organises specialised training programmes and lectures to broaden the education of the Institute's monks and students.

The programme includes:

- **Buddhist scholarship in dialogue with modernity** — visiting masters of Buddhist philosophy are invited to give teachings tailored to the contemporary context.
- **Public lectures** on subjects of social and ethical relevance, given by invited experts.
- **Twenty-first-century leadership training**, supported by the [Khyentse Foundation](https://khyentsefoundation.org/).
- **Modern pedagogical methods** suited to the contemporary mind.
- **Buddhist textual research training** under the [ARPI](https://www.kc-tbts.uni-hamburg.de/en/research/projects.html) project of the Khyentse Center for Tibetan Buddhist Textual Scholarship at the University of Hamburg, Germany.

These workshops, led by international scholars, are an ongoing part of the Institute's educational offering.
