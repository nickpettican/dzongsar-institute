**Enrollment of foreign students.**

Those students not from Tibet, India, Nepal and Bhutan fall under the category of foreign student. The basic criteria of eligibility to enter the Shedra are [common to all students](/en/education/study/new-students/). Foreign students get the help from Dzongsar Shedra in following ways:

* An admission letter from the Shedra to get student visa and
* A letter to get PAP documents to stay in Tibetan settlements.

However, due to constraints from the Indian government it is unlikely that a student visa will be granted as of now. We are currently working on a solution, by which time we will update this page.
