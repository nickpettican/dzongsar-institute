The Publications Department of Dzongsar Khyentse Chökyi Lodrö Institute oversees the editorial, design, and printing of the Institute's books, journals, and teaching materials — including newly composed works, scriptural reprints, syllabus volumes, and commemorative publications.

The department works closely with the Editorial Department and produces both Tibetan-language scholarly editions and selected English and Chinese translations.
