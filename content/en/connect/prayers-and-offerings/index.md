Dzongsar Khyentse Chökyi Lodrö Institute performs a variety of pujas (ritual offerings and prayer services) for the welfare of all beings. The monastic community accepts requests for specific dedications.

### Principal categories

- **Recurring pujas** — annual practices including aspiration prayers, the Medicine Buddha sādhana, and others.
- **Healing pujas** — for recovery from illness.
- **Obstacle-clearing pujas** — including Mahākāla sādhanas.
- **Memorial pujas** — for the benefit of the deceased.

### Requesting an offering

To request a dedicated prayer or puja at Dzongsar, please reach out through our [contact page](/en/connect/contact/).
