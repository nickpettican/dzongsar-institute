### College Calendar

1. Commemorating the founding day of Shedra on the fifteenth of first month of Tibetan calendar.
2. Classes for the academic year start on the twenty first day of the first Tibetan month, which marks the death anniversary of both Gorampa Sonam Sengye and Jamyang Khyentse Wangpo.
3. Memorial for the death anniversary of Khenchen Kunga Wangchuk on the twentieth day of the third Tibetan month, with expositions, dialectical debates and compositions.
4. Memorial for the death anniversary of Khyentse Chokyi Lodoe on the sixth day of the fourth Tibetan month, with expositions, dialectical debates and compositions.
5. Celebration of the birthday of Kyabje Jamyang Khyentse Thubten Chokyi Gyatso (Dzongsar Jamyang Khyentse Rinpoche) on the 18th of June.
6. Monsoon retreat observance from the fifteenth day of the sixth Tibetan month to the thirtieth day of the seventh Tibetan month.
7. Certificates for Madhyama, Shastra and Acharya degrees given on the twenty first day of the first Tibetan month upon students’ successful completion of these courses.
8. Teachings on Tantra by renowned masters—subject to availability—organized every year.
9. Lecture sessions and workshops on diverse cultures by renowned scholars—subject to availability—organized every year.
