### Our Vision

The profound intention throughout the various manifestations of the great Rime master Kyabje Jamyang Khyentse is to propagate the teachings of Shakyamuni Buddha—the only source of benefits and happiness—, to make the study and practice of the Buddhadharma available for those who are interested, and to help generating many individuals deeply learned in the Buddhadharma during this degenerative time: a time in which people have become enslaved by materialistic pursuits, and spiritual and humanistic values like contentment, love, compassion and trust are rarely given prominence.
