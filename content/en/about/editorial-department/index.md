### The literary committee

The literary committee was first set up in 2008 as per the decisions of  the administration. It was headed by a Khenpo with three assistants. In the same year they published a magazine commemorating the 25 years of Dzongsar Shedra in India. Since then till 2016 the committee had edited and published nine annual Shedra’s magazine, ‘Chokyi Lodoe’. It had also transcribed, edited and publish teachings of Kyabje Khyentse Rinpoche.

In 2015, as ordered by the principal of the Shedra, the committee finished editing the fourteen volume works of late Khenchen Kunga Wangchuck. The committee has until now been responsible for all works of editions and publications of the Shedra.
